Environmental

This is our planet – let's take care of it.
Protecting the environment is the greatest challenge our society faces right now. We are changing our climate in ways future generations will have a hard time coping with. There are big challenges which need to be addressed world-wide on the political level, but peoples' individual actions are also a key part in making the change. This problem is so big it needs to be addressed by all possible means.

I believe we should think about what we consume before we consume it. We prepare and eat food on a daily basis, so I decided to tackle the problem of food waste right in my own household. TO complete the natural cycle of leftovers becoming fertilizer again, I built my own worm composting system.

Here is an economic analysis I did on the Greenhouse Gas Emissions of the Canadian Industrial Sector for the Apps4ClimateAction competition.
